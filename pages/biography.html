Biography

Dr. Vishwanath Bijalwan is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at Amity University, Punjab, India. He has more than 13 years of teaching and research experience in computer vision, human gait analysis, soft robotics, and deep learning. He has authored 26 SCI-indexed publications along with several other peer-reviewed works.

He received a DAAD AI Net Fellowship (04/2024) and the 2021 Uttarakhand State Government Excellence Award for Research. His broader research interests include human activity recognition, biomedical signal processing, and intelligent robotic systems.
