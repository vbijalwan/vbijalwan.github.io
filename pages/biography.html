Biography

Dr. Vishwanath Bijalwan is Dr. Vishwanath Bijalwan is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at Amity University, Punjab, India. He has over 13 years of teaching experience and is an accomplished researcher in computer vision, soft robotics, and deep learning, with 23 SCI-indexed publications. He received a DAAD AI NET Fellowship (04/2024) and the 2021 Uttarakhand State Government Excellence Award for Research.

Education

Ph.D., ICFAI University, Dehradun (2022). M.Tech (Honours), Dehradun Institute of Technology. B.Tech ECE.

Employment

  • Previous roles include Post-Doctoral Fellow, Assistant Professor, and Research positions.
