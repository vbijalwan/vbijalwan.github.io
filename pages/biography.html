Biography

Vishwanath Bijalwan is a Post-Doctoral researcher and academic with expertise in Computer Vision, NLP, Human Gait analysis, Soft Robotics and Wearable Sensors. He has held positions as Postdoctoral Fellow at Hanbat National University, Assistant Professor at SR University and Associate Professor at Amity University.
