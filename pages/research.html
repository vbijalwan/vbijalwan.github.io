Research Interests

My research focuses on intersections of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Human Gait analysis, Soft Robotics, Shape Memory Alloy actuators, and wearable sensors. I am interested in explainable AI and applications of deep learning in rehabilitation robotics and biomedical signal processing.
