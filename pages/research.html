Research Interests

My research focuses on Computer Vision, Soft Robotics, Shape Memory Alloy (SMA) actuators, wearable sensors, human gait analysis, and explainable deep learning for biomedical, rehabilitation, and intelligent robotic applications.

Selected Research Projects

  • Real-Time Neuroadaptive Rehabilitation Systems for Gait and Posture Correction
  • Explainable AI for Detection and Classification of Pathological Patterns in Medical Imaging
  • AI-Enabled Early Gait Disorder Detection using Wearable Sensors and SMA-based Prosthetics
