Personal

About my family

I am Vishwanath, born on November 07, 1985 in a Hindu Brahmin family. My birthplace is Uttarkashi, a city closely associated with Lord Vishwanath, the “Swami of the whole world.”

My village is Idak, which comes under Banal Patti, a beautiful village situated on the bank of the river Yamuna. Growing up in this environment has deeply influenced my values and connection to nature and spirituality.

My father is now retired from government service, and my mother is a homemaker. Whatever we have become today is largely because of her ambitions, dedication, and countless sacrifices. I am always grateful to my parents for their support and guidance.

Hobbies & Interests

I enjoy visiting beautiful places, especially in Uttarakhand, and have also spent memorable time in Daejeon (South Korea), Erlangen and Nuremberg (Germany). Traveling and meeting new people from different cultures is something I truly enjoy.

My main passion, however, is research. I am particularly interested in:

  • Human gait analysis
  • Soft robotics
  • Computer vision
  • Natural language processing

These areas inspire me to keep learning, exploring new ideas, and contributing to the scientific community.
