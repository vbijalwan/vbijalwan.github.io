Academic Profile

Research-driven teaching, innovation, and applied AI leadership

Dr. Vishwanath Bijalwan works on the development of intelligent systems that connect AI algorithms with real-world sensing, control, and decision-making environments. His research includes multimodal sensor fusion using cameras, LiDAR, IMU, and GPS, with applications in autonomous aerial systems, robotics, human activity analysis, and AI-powered perception.

Alongside research, he contributes actively to academic training through invited talks, expert sessions, faculty development programs, and workshops on AI-assisted research, data analysis, visualization, scientific writing, and advanced machine learning.
