Associate Professor of Computer Science at Amity University, Punjab, India, bridging cutting-edge AI research with real-world autonomous systems.

My research focuses on integrating AI algorithms with real-world sensing and control systems, including multimodal sensor fusion using cameras, LiDAR, IMU, and GPS. I work on autonomous aerial systems, human activity analysis, intelligent robotics, and AI-driven perception and control frameworks, with emphasis on responsible and ethical AI.

I actively contribute to the academic community through invited and expert talks, faculty development programs, and workshops at national and international institutions, covering AI-assisted research, data analysis and visualization, multimodal UAV perception, and advanced machine learning techniques.
